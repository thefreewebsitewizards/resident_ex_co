About The Resident Experience Co.

The Resident Experience Co. is a full-service event planning company that takes care of every detail. From birthday parties and pickleball tournaments to resident appreciation events and corporate team building—we do it all. Our goal is simple: to create memorable experiences while taking the stress off your plate.

We bring together entertainment, planning, and logistics in one place so you can relax and enjoy your event.

Our Services

From intimate gatherings to large-scale events, we handle every detail with care.

Birthday Parties

Celebrate in style with our complete party planning services for kids and adults alike.

  • Face painting, magicians, clowns & balloon artists
  • Mermaids, princess characters & themed entertainment
  • Bounce houses, outdoor games & boat rentals
  • Custom cakes & complete catering

Corporate Events

Strengthen your team with engaging activities and seamless event execution.

  • Custom team-building & ice-breaker activities
  • Wellness events (yoga, fitness challenges)
  • Holiday parties & corporate celebrations
  • Full logistics & venue coordination

Resident Events

Create vibrant communities with year-round engaging activities for residents.

  • Event calendar creation & yearly scheduling
  • Seasonal parties, festivals & appreciation events
  • Communication templates for residents
  • Vendor coordination & permit handling

Sports Events

Get active with our professionally organized sports clinics and tournaments - kids and adults welcome!

  • Pickleball clinics & tournaments (all skill levels)
  • Ideal for apartment communities & corporate outings
  • All equipment provided
  • Branded prizes & medals

Entertainment

Elevate your event with professional entertainers and unique experiences.

  • Face painters & balloon artists
  • DJs, live music & photo booths
  • Mermaids & themed characters
  • Inflatables & interactive games

Food & Catering

Delight your guests with delicious food options tailored to your event.

  • Local vendor coordination
  • Full-service or buffet-style
  • Themed treats & food trucks
